#+title: Workshop on Geometric Analysis and Partial Differential Equations
#+options: toc:nil


The workshop is about geometric analysis and partial differential
equations. We hope to bring researchers in the field of geometric analysis
and partial differential equations together to communicate both their works
and ideas.
