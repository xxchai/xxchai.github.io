1. ZHOU, Hengyu (周恒宇)

Title: A blow-up method to the Dirichlet problem of prescribed mean curvature equations.

Date: Date: 10-11AM, 27 Feb (KST)

Zoom link: https://us06web.zoom.us/j/86733316671

Abstract: This talk introduces a blow-up method of prescribed mean curvature (PMC) equations. We propose an NC-f condition inspired from Schoen-Yau's constructions on Jang graphs. With the NC-f property we establish the Dirichlet problems of a large class of PMC equations on Riemannian manifolds. Curvature estimates of PMC hypersurfaces during the blow-up are also presented.

2. HONG, HAN (洪寒)

Title: Large Steklov eigenvalue on hyperbolic surfaces

Zoom link: https://us06web.zoom.us/j/81578164584

Date: 2:30-3:30 PM, 27 Feb (KST)

Abstract: In this work, we construct a sequence of compact hyperbolic surfaces with connected geodesic boundary component such that its Steklov spectrum blows up. Moreover, we show that this property is generic in the moduli space by using Mirzakhani’s integration formula.

5. ZHU, Jintian (朱锦天)

Date: 2:30-4:00 pm, 28 Feb (KST)

Zoom link: https://us06web.zoom.us/j/83942403977

Title: Gauss-Bonnet formula beyond the aspherical conjecture

Abstract: In this talk, I'll make a discussion on recent progress in the research of scalar curvature. I'll review the backgrounds from Gauss-Bonnet formula to the aspherical conjecture, and then focus on the following Gauss-Bonnet inequality: if the universal covering of a closed manifold with nonnegative scalar curvature has "homological dimension" two, then either it is flat or its Gauss-Bonnet quantity is no greater than \(8\pi\).
